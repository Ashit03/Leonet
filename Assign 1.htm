RIMS

Rourkela Institute of Management Studies (RIMS), Established in the year 1984, it is counted among the top Business schools of India. Situated in the culturally-diverse city of Rourkela, RIMS, as an educational institution, has not only empowered people living in this city but across the state of Odisha through quality education and several social welfare initiatives since its inception.
